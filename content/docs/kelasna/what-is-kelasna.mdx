**Kelasna** is Sawala Cloud's **nano-learning marketplace**. Selling organizations author short, modular courses; learners browse a public catalog, enrol, work through bite-size lessons, and earn a certificate on completion. It lives at **`kelasna.id`**.

## When to choose Kelasna

Reach for Kelasna when you want to package knowledge into short courses:

- Onboarding or training delivered as bite-size lessons.
- A public catalog of courses people can discover and enrol in.
- Certificates that recognize learners who finish.

## Core concepts

- **Course** — authored by a selling organization, organized into **modules** and **lessons**. A course can be a draft or published, and public or unlisted.
- **Enrollment** — a learner joins a course. Learners sign in with their own accounts, separate from the organizations that author courses.
- **Completion & certificate** — as a learner finishes lessons, their progress is tracked; finishing the course earns a certificate.

<Callout type="info">
In the current release every course is **free** to enrol in. Paid courses are planned, so the catalog and enrollment model already account for pricing.
</Callout>

## Two sides

Kelasna serves two audiences: **learners**, who browse and take courses on the public marketplace, and **sellers**, who author and manage their courses. The same platform is designed to power public marketplaces, white-label storefronts, and private company academies.
