**Sawala Cloud** is a platform of independent but connected building blocks for building websites, apps, and digital products. Instead of stitching together a separate CMS, a separate file host, a separate form service, and a separate billing tool, you get them all under **one account**, managed from **one dashboard**, and reachable through **one set of APIs**.

Each building block is a **product** you can adopt on its own:

- **[Kontena](/en/docs/kontena/what-is-kontena)** — a headless CMS for structured content (articles, pages, catalogs).
- **[Berkasna](/en/docs/berkasna/what-is-berkasna)** — file and asset storage with public and private files.
- **[Formulir](/en/docs/formulir/what-is-formulir)** — a form builder with a submission inbox you can embed anywhere.

…plus a growing family of other products (hosting, transactional email, structured data, and more — see [Products overview](/en/docs/general-concepts/products-overview)).

## The shape of the platform

Three ideas hold everything together:

1. **You sign in once.** One Sawala Cloud account gives you access to every product. See [Accounts & sign-in](/en/docs/general-concepts/accounts-and-sign-in).
2. **Your work lives in projects.** An **organization** is your workspace and where billing lives; a **project** is an isolated space inside it. Content, files, and forms all belong to a project. See [Organizations & projects](/en/docs/general-concepts/organizations-and-projects).
3. **Everything has an API.** Each product exposes a REST API. You read and write from a website, a mobile app, the command line, or a server — using a project-scoped [API key](/en/docs/general-concepts/api-keys-and-access).

## Who it's for

- **Editorial and marketing teams** who manage content without touching code.
- **Developers** who want backend building blocks without operating servers — everything runs on Sawala's infrastructure.
- **Agencies and product teams** who run many client sites or projects from one account, each isolated in its own project.

## Two ways to use it

- **The dashboard** at [sawala.cloud/dashboard](https://sawala.cloud/dashboard) — a visual interface for editors and admins to manage schemas, content, files, forms, members, and keys.
- **The APIs** — for developers to pull content into a site or push data in from their own apps.

Most teams use both: editors work in the dashboard, developers consume the APIs.
