### Embed the form on your site

Paste this into any HTML page on your site (swap `slug` for your form's slug):

```html
<iframe
  src="https://formulir.id/embed/your-form-slug"
  style="width: 100%; height: 600px; border: 0;"
  loading="lazy"
></iframe>
```

For deeper integration (form rendered in your DOM, not an iframe), see [how to embed a form](/en/docs/formulir/how-to-embed-form).
