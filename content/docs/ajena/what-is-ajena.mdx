**Ajena** is Sawala Cloud's **AI chat agent**. You upload your own documents to a knowledge base, embed a chat UI on your site, and your visitors chat with an assistant whose answers are grounded in *your* content — not generic web knowledge. Each visitor gets their own conversation history.

## When to choose Ajena

Reach for Ajena when you want an assistant that knows your material:

- A support or help assistant that answers from your documentation.
- A product or sales assistant grounded in your catalog and FAQs.
- An internal knowledge assistant over your team's documents.

## How it works

- **Knowledge base** — admins upload documents. Ajena indexes them so the assistant can retrieve the most relevant passages when answering.
- **Grounded answers** — at chat time, Ajena finds the relevant pieces of your knowledge base and gives them to the language model as context, so replies stay tied to your content.
- **Sessions** — each user has their own list of conversations, with message history preserved. Session links reveal nothing about who owns them.

<Callout type="info">
Answers stream back live as they're generated, so the assistant feels responsive even on long replies.
</Callout>

## Adding it to your site

Ajena provides a ready-made chat UI you embed on your site, plus an admin surface for managing the documents in your knowledge base. Your Sawala sign-in carries over to it — see [Accounts & sign-in](/id/docs/general-concepts/accounts-and-sign-in).
